About Me

Growing up in New Zealand, I spent a lot of my time focused on my passion for creative writing and english literature - it was only on my return to England in 2019 where I undertook an A-level in computer science that I realised how inherently creative the subject was and began to consider it as a viable career path.

I'm still trying to discover where I'll fit in the diverse and exciting industry that technology has helped form, but my current interests are front-end development, software engineering and testing. I'm constantly looking for opportunies to grow and develop, and hope whoever is reading this can come along on that journey with me!
